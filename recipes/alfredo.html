Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

A staple Italian dish, made from a creamy sauce this dish accents flavors of its rich creamy texture,cheese and garlic in this recipe we'll be adding a few additonal ingridients a personal recipe that goes outside the classic/traditional alfredo sauce and adds a layer of spice and sweetness to the dish.

Ingridients

  • Fettuccine Noodles (homemade or packaged)
  • Half and Half Cream
  • Garlic
  • Honey
  • Cajun Spice
  • Butter
  • Cornstarch
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Making a sauce

    2. Add 1/2 Cups of cream into a saucepot, heated on a low simmer adding an additonal 1tsp of butter into the sauce, whisking until butter is melted/incorperated

  2. Enhancing the Sauce

    3. Mince up a clove of garlic, finely minced and add into the sauce. Add 1tsp of honey,a pinch of cajun spice and mix in thoroughly and let it simmer while you boil your pasta. At this stage its optional to add a pinch of cornstarch this will thicken the sauce, when adding ensure you whisk it rapidly to avoid clumping.

  3. Boil Pasta

    4. Boil your pasta until ready/desired texture

  4. Add sauce onto pasta

    5. Strain your pasta and then pour the sauce onto the noodles, serve with parmesan cheese topping
