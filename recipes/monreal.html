Montreal Sandwich

The Montreal Smoked Sandwich is a classic Sandwich found in most of Quebec its savory and uniqe smokey flavor create a great combination of mouthwatering flavors,they simply are unique to this Sandwich. Its simple preparation and delicous results are a sure winner and make it a staple in much of Quebec.

Ingridients

  • Montreal Smoked Meat
  • Mustard
  • Bread
  • White Onions
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Creating the Sandwich

    2. In a frying pan layout the smoked meat,adding thinly sliced white onions to fry. once the onions have started to fry add 1tsp of honey and stir it into the mixture making a sweet and savory mixture. (Add desired amount of onions/meat based on preference)

  2. Plating

    3. Toast your desired sandwich bread, adding mustard to both inner sides before adding the meat/onion mixture once completed cut in half and serve.
